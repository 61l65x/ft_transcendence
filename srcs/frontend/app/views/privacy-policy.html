Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our app. This is a student project and not intended for commercial use.

Information We Collect

We only collect your data that is essential for the functionality of the app: (1) username, (2) email address, (3) first name, (4) last name, (5) avatar. Except for username, other data is optional. You may choose not to provide this additional information, and it will not affect your ability to use the app.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to enhance your experience within the app, such as managing user accounts and personalizing content. We may use cookies to help you stay logged in. You have the option to disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect your experience with the app

Your Data Privacy Rights

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. For this app, the most relevant rights you have regarding your data are:

  • right to access: view your personal data on profile page;
  • right to rectify: edit your personal data on profile page if it is inaccurate or incomplete;
  • right to erasure: anonymize your personal data and disable your account, or delete your account entirely. Both actions will remove your personal data from our system.

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, as this is a student project, we recommend that you do not provide any sensitive or personally identifiable information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and by continuing to use the app, you agree to be bound by the revised policy. We recommend that you review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your data privacy rights, please contact us.
